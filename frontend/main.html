ABOUT US

Who Are We ?

MSP Tech Club is a student activity organized by Microsoft student partners under the supervision of Microsoft in the Faculty of Computer Science at Cairo University and We are the Link between The company and the student,Our MSP Tech Club Started in 2011 and helps about 200 students every year to gain more experience.

Our Vision ?

Increase the knowledge of students by providing them with the essential training and the interpersonal skills. Projecting the conceptual plans on the reality and applicability levels are our duty. We are in the helping for whoever wants the assistance and support. Changing the mentality of our nation what we are doing

Our Aim ?

The team's main goal is to reduce the gap between students and the labor market, and to have workshops available to all students from all colleges and universities.

What We Do ?

teaching students and gain them experience by organizing workshops in different fields, It's Totally for free in collaboration with Microsoft by Our committees "Academic , Digital Marketing, Design & Media, Fundraising & Events, HR, IT, Logistics and Public Relations" and Our Participants fields "Interaction Design, Marketing, Mobile app, Soft Skills and web development".
